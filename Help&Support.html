Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I report a bug?

    To report a bug, visit the bug reporting page and provide details about the bug, including its title, description, and severity.

  • Can I track the status of reported bugs?

    Yes, you can track the status of reported bugs on the bug tracking page. Each bug will be assigned a status that indicates its progress towards resolution.
